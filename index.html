Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our image compression tool

Is Imagify really free?

Yes! Our basic image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden costs. We may offer premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No. All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What image formats do you support?

We support JPG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can choose to keep the original format or convert to another format during compression.

Does it work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Imagify is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets as well as desktop computers.
